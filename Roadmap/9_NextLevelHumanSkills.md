## Next Level Human Skills For the Next 250-500 Years

It's an obvious cliche which everyone can understand if they give it a moment or two of though, but in the next 250 to 500 years, we are going to **HAVE TO** *get along* better politically and culturally ... it's not optional or something that'd be nice to do -- it's really a **HAVE TO** do ... or otherwise, projects of this spectacularly gigantic scale will topple like the tower of Babel on those who are attempting to participate in building and coordinating this massive undertaking. 

# Questions for Developing High-Agency Individuals and Teams

250 questions help us ***get started*** in the contemplation of team building, interdisciplinary thinking creative exploration necessary to explore an impossibly difficult project like Quantum Life. The STARTING context is really terribly important, just to get us STARTED in the correct-ish direction ... that's why it necessary to read through and think ***superficially*** about all 250 questions before drilling deeper into practical engineering concerns and philosophical implications of the entire topic.
